Unit 5 — Capstone

Weeks 16–16 · 1 week

What you'll learn

The Capstone is a single sustained project: a retail sales dataset of about 5,000 rows. You'll clean it, explore it, ask three business questions, answer them with code and visuals, and present your findings. There are no new concepts in Unit 5 — only integration. This is the deliverable you'll add to your portfolio and show in interviews.
