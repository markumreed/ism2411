USF provides free DataCamp for Classrooms access for this course. Eight courses are required and aligned to weekly topics. Two more are optional for students who want to go further.

Access: You'll get an invite link to your USF email in Week 1. Accept the invite to activate your free account. No credit card required, no expiration after the course.

Tips for working through DataCamp

  • Don't skip the slides. The video content explains the why behind the syntax. The exercises only test the how.
  • Type the code, don't copy-paste. Your fingers learn syntax in a way your eyes can't.
  • Take notes in a single file per course — w02_datacamp_notes.md, etc. You'll thank yourself before the midterm.
  • If you finish early, don't wait. Push ahead. Front-loading these is a survival strategy for finals season.

How completion is verified

The instructor has access to a DataCamp Groups dashboard showing your progress. Course completion is automatic and visible in real time — no submission required from you. If a course shows as incomplete on the dashboard after its deadline, that's a zero for that course.

If a DataCamp course goes down or there's a platform issue: screenshot the error and email the instructor that day. Don't wait until the deadline.
